Don Juan and the Devil

Don Juan in Hell is a part of the four-act play, Man and Superman written by George Bernard Shaw. It’s a debate and a philosophical dialog happening in hell. The participants are Don Juan, his former lover and her father, and the Devil himself. Below is part of the conversation between Don Juan and the Devil.
